Davis Weather Station

The complete definition of this Davis Weather Station can be found at http://vast.uah.edu/downloads/sensorML/v1.0/examples/sensors/DavisWeather/NSSTC-WX01.xml.

To begin editing the DavisWeatherStation.rng, select the root element in the tree: SensorML. Right click on the mouse and a popup menu will appear. Follow the menu and select "sml.System".
