System

After adding the System element, you may have to unfold the SensorML folder to view the addition to the tree. Highlight the tree entry for member:System. In the properties view, you will now see the SensorML attributes and elements that can be included in an instance that complies of SensorML Schema 1.0.1. We will fill in the gml:id and gml:description.

NOTE: The windows may be rearranged, docked, and undocked according to your wishes.
